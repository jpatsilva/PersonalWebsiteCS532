Hobbies

Golf

History

I have been playing golf since I was six years old. My dad gave me two clubs and I played with those two clubs for a year or two. A local man sold my dad a set of old golf clubs for me. My dad had them cut down to my size and I played with those clubs for the next few years. Between my 8th grade and 9th grade years, my dad bought me my first new set of golf clubs, Ben Hogan Apex II . I played with these clubs until just a few years ago. With my kids off to college, I decided to play more golf. I bought a new set of golf clubs in 2019. I bought a set of Titleist irons.

Golf Tournaments

I have a good friend from my home town. His name is Gary. We have played a lot of golf in the last three years. We've Played in Arizona, Nevada, and all around New Mexico. We've played in golf tournaments at New Mexico State University Golf Course and Picacho Hills Country Club in Las Cruces.

Grass

I enjoy everything about my grass. I recently purchased a reel mower . A reel mower usually has 5 to 10 blades which cut grass in a scissor action. The reel blades rotate and scissor the grass blades
between the reel blade and a bed knife that is fixed to the height you want the grass cut. Generally, the more blades the lower the cut.
